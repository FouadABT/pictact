Welcome to the Urban Architecture Challenge! Your mission is to capture stunning architectural elements that define our modern cityscapes.

Look for the following specific elements:

  • Art Deco facades - Buildings with decorative geometric patterns
  • Modern glass structures - Contemporary buildings with prominent glass features
  • Historic stone archways - Classical architectural arches in stone
  • Unique rooftop designs - Creative or unusual rooftop architecture

Each submission will be reviewed for accuracy and artistic merit. Bonus points awarded for exceptional composition and lighting!
